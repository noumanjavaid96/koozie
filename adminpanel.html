    • Use the platforms tools to input your script and set up the conversation flow. Most platforms offer templates to make this easier.

    • Before going live, test the chatbot to ensure it responds correctly and provides helpful information.

  6. Launch and Monitor:

    • Once youre satisfied, launch the chatbot on your website or social media. Keep an eye on its performance and make adjustments as needed based on customer interactions.

  7. Provide a Human Option:

    • Ensure theres an easy way for customers to reach you directly if the chatbot cant answer their questions.
